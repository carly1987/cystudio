what is a

PEDICAB?

Pedicabs are an alternative way of transportation, environmentally friendly tricycles propelled by pedaling. Pedicab rides are a new and unique experience where you can enjoy a comfortable ride, the sun, the breeze, and the cultural environments that surround the city.

what is

SPOKES?

As Oak Cliff grows, so does the need for parking and public transportation. While solutions to the growing need for parking and public transportation are in the works, Spokes Eclectic Pedicab Rides offers an immediate solution by providing a service that is not only green and affordable, but a fun and unique service to the bike-friendly, community-centric culture of Oak Cliff.

THE KESSLER THEATER

The historic Kessler Theater opened in the spring of 1942. Owned and operated by Gene Autry from the late 1940s to the early 1950s and nearly destroyed by a 1957 tornado, the Kessler has undergone many renovations throughout the years. Currently operated as a mix-use facility, the theater books live music shows throughout the year as well as operates as a special events venue.

ODDFELLOWS

Oddfellows is a favorite hot spot for those visiting the Bishop Arts District. The farmhouse-chic cafe, founded by Oak Cliff community leaders, lures people in with its fantastic coffee program and then backs it all up with its excellent variety of traditional American dishes with ingredients that are primarily locally sourced. Oddfellows is so passionate about their coffee program that they went out and purchased a rare, La Marzocco Strada, expresso machine which is only the fourth in existence in the country.

7TH STREET MURALS

The 7th Street Murals is an ongoing program that is continually evolving and growing with the Oak Cliff community. The program involves professional muralists mentoring students and teaching young artists while at the same time adding artwork that lends to neighborhood beautification and countering graffiti. These large artwork installments have created a wonderful outdoor gallery that will constantly grab visitors attention.

ENO'S PIZZA TAVERN

Eno's, run by Chef-Owner Matt Spillers, is located in the Bishop Arts District and has come to be known for its legendary thin-crust pizza and its varied selection of microbrews and craft beers. Eno's makes it a point to buy ingredients and produce from local farmers. As for their beverage program, they look for brews and wines made by American craftsmen.

HISTORIC TEXAS THEATRE

The Texas Theatre, a historical landmark, is praised for its vintage atmosphere, art deco, indie films, local concerts and a full bar that lets patrons play LP records. The theatre has a significant place in the history of Dallas. It was opened in 1931 by billionaire Howard Hughes, and then in 1963, Lee Harvey Oswald was captured there. It has changed ownership many times in the years it has been open, but in 2001, the Oak Cliff Foundation restored and renovated the building.

F.A.Q

You Have Questions. We Have Answers.

If you have a specific question not listed here please contact us.

What is a Pedicab?

A small pedal-operated vehicle, serving as a taxi. (aka: bike taxi, bike rickshaw)

How can I catch a ride?

Just like any other taxi, give us a shout and we'll come by and pick you up. We can also arrange for special pick up services ahead of time if you give us a call.

Where do you ride?

Right now we primarily service N. Oak Cliff in and around Bishop Arts. Please see our route map for details.

What services do pedicabs provide?

Spokes Pedicabs provides many services. Taxi is the primary service but we are also available for weddings, birthdays, pub crawls, advertising opportunities, and more.

How many people can ride in a pedicab?

We can carry two to three adults.

Is riding in the street safe?

Pedicabs, just as regular bicycles, are required to operate in the street by Texas Law. We are well lit at night and even have brake and turn signals.

How much does a pedicab ride cost?

Rides are free although tips are appreciated.

How can I advertise on a pedicab?

There are many different areas of all sizes to advertise on each pedicab. We are glad to provide a new form of advertising to the area. Give us a call and we would be happy to work with you.
